What's inside

Screen & camera capture

Record screen with the iOS broadcast extension, or roll camera and screen at the same time. Every clip is timestamped on the frame so the evidence is unambiguous.

Annotated feedback

Pin notes, voice memos, photos, and screenshots to a session timeline. Auto-attach device model, OS, app version, and optional battery / network state.

Charge-port diagnostics

Quick built-in tester for charging behaviour — wattage, state-of-charge curve, and anomalies — captured into the same report stream as the rest of your testing.

AI-assisted reports

Turn raw feedback into a polished bug report or testing summary. Defaults to Apple Intelligence on-device on iPhone 15 Pro and newer; bring your own key for Anthropic, OpenAI, Gemini, or DeepSeek.

Confidential Mode

One toggle locks the app to on-device processing only. All cloud sync, share, and cloud-AI flows are blocked while it's on. Designed for NDA-bound work.

Cloud sync — your account

Optional sync to your own Google Drive, OneDrive, or iCloud. We never run a backend, never see your files, never act as a middleman.

Privacy by default

BetaSuite is built local-first. Feedback entries, recordings, and project data live on your device, encrypted at rest. Data only leaves your device when you use a feature that calls outside — cloud sync to your own account, or an AI provider you've selected. Read the full privacy policy.
