What's inside

Dual recording — screen + PiP camera

Capture the screen and the device itself at the same time. Show developers exactly what the user did and how the app behaved in real time.

Instant capture (15 / 30 s replay)

Background-record the last 15 or 30 seconds. A bug already happened? Tap once to save the buffer.

Annotate photos and video

Draw, pin notes, and mark up media right in the app. Every frame and photo carries a to-the-second timestamp; imported media keeps its original timestamp too.

Voice memos, transcribed on-device

Speak observations as you test. On supported iPhones, Apple's Speech framework transcribes locally — the audio never leaves your device.

AI session summaries and reports

When a session ends, BetaSuite turns markers, notes, and media into a clean bullet summary. On iPhone 15 Pro / 16 / 17 with Apple Intelligence, the inference runs on-device.

Confidential Mode for NDA work

One toggle blocks cloud sync and every cloud AI provider — only Apple's on-device paths run. Test sensitive products without changing how you work.

How it works

1. Record the session

Start a project, hit record, and use the app or device the way a tester would.

2. Mark and annotate

Drop markers, add voice notes, and draw on screenshots — all timestamped automatically.

3. Generate a report

BetaSuite assembles a session summary plus the media, ready to share with developers.

Privacy-first by design

BetaSuite leans on on-device AI wherever your iPhone supports it.

AI that stays on your device

Apple Intelligence (iOS 26+, iPhone 15 Pro / 16 / 17) handles text generation locally.

Speech that never leaves

Voice memos transcribe on-device via Apple's Speech framework on supported iPhones.

One toggle for sensitive work

Confidential Mode blocks every cloud route — sync, AI, and analytics — so NDA testing stays local.
