Frequently asked questions

How much does BetaSuite cost?
$9.99 USD as a one-time purchase. There are no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no ads.
Which devices are supported?
iPhone (iOS 16 or later) is the primary platform. Android is supported via the same Flutter build. Some features — Apple Intelligence, Apple WeatherKit, the Broadcast Upload Extension for screen recording — are iOS-only.
How do I delete my data?
BetaSuite is a local-first app — there is no user account and no server-side data. To wipe everything: open BetaSuite, go to Settings → Data → Wipe all app data. Uninstalling the app also removes all on-device data. For files you've synced to your own Google Drive, OneDrive, or iCloud account, delete them directly from that account.
Do I need to create an account?
No. BetaSuite never asks you to register. Your data is generated and stored only on your device, encrypted at rest. You only sign in to a third party (Google, Microsoft, Apple) if you opt in to cloud sync.
How do I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by the App Store. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and select your BetaSuite purchase. We don't process payments directly, so we can't issue refunds on Apple's behalf.
Why does BetaSuite need camera, microphone, and location permissions?
Camera and microphone are used for capturing video, audio notes, and screen + camera dual recording. Location is used only when you turn on the GPS overlay so a captured frame shows where it was taken. Each permission is requested on first use, with an explanation, and can be revoked at any time in iOS / Android Settings.
What is Confidential Mode?
A toggle that locks the app to on-device processing only. While it's on: cloud sync is disabled, every cloud AI provider is blocked, and a confirmation dialog appears before any share or export. Apple Intelligence (which runs on-device) is still permitted. Use it for NDA-bound testing.
Where do AI requests go?
Default: Apple Intelligence on-device when your iPhone supports it (iOS 26+, iPhone 15 Pro and newer); otherwise Google Firebase AI as a cloud fallback. You can switch to bring-your-own-key for Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, Google Gemini, or DeepSeek — in which case the request goes directly from your device to that provider with your key, never via us. See the privacy policy for the full picture.
Screen recording isn't working — what should I check?
On iOS, screen recording uses a Broadcast Upload Extension. On the first use you need to tap "BetaSuiteBroadcast" in the system broadcast picker and then "Start Broadcast". If the picker doesn't show BetaSuiteBroadcast, restart the device — iOS sometimes caches the extension list. On Android, grant the System Alert Window permission for the floating record button to appear.
How do I report a bug?
In-app: Settings → Legal → Contact Us → category "Bug Report". Or email betasuiteau@gmail.com. Please include device model, iOS / Android version, BetaSuite version (Settings → About), and steps to reproduce. A short screen recording attached as a file is incredibly helpful.
Will my old data survive an app update?
Yes. BetaSuite uses Hive with a compatibility-preserving schema — every field added in a newer version is read with a null-fallback, so older recordings, projects, and feedback stay accessible after an update. If you ever need to migrate to a new device, use Settings → Data → Export to create a portable backup first.

Status & outages

Because BetaSuite runs locally with no backend, there's nothing on our side to go down. If a feature that depends on a third party fails — Apple WeatherKit, a cloud-storage provider, an AI vendor — check that provider's status page directly. We'll post a notice on the home page if a widespread issue affects multiple users.

Still stuck? Email betasuiteau@gmail.com with your device model, OS version, and BetaSuite version (Settings → About). We respond within 2 business days.
